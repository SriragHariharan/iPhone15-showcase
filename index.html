About Us
Founded in 1976 by the trailblazing trio of Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple Inc. embarked on an extraordinary journey that would leave an indelible mark on the world of technology. Born out of a humble garage in Cupertino, California, Apple quickly rose to prominence, driven by a shared vision of innovation and a passion for pushing the boundaries of what was thought possible. From the groundbreaking release of the Apple I and Apple II computers to the revolutionary Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, the company consistently redefined entire industries with its visionary products and user-centric design philosophy. Alongside its iconic hardware, Apple's software innovations, such as macOS, iOS, and the App Store, have transformed the way people interact with technology, seamlessly integrating innovation into everyday life. Beyond its product offerings, Apple's commitment to sustainability, corporate responsibility, and philanthropy has further solidified its position as a global leader, inspiring millions around the world to think differently and dream big.
